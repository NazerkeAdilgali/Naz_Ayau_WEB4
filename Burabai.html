Burabai National Park

Simply, the Kokshetau district in Northern Kazakhstan was named Kazakh Switzerland. A huge wall of forest comes from nowhere right in the endless distance of the steppe. The green veil reveals a fascinating landscape. Fish lakes, mountains piercing the sky, rocks, coniferous, green trees that have seen more than one century in their lives - all this can be seen by passing the forest fence. A great landscape, gorgeous views conceals Lake Burabay in Kazakhstan or, in another way, Borovoe. Burabai is an amazing place for nature lovers.Okzhetpes Rock is the most beautiful rock of Burabaya.
